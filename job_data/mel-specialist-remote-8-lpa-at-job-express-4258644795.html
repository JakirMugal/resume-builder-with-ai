      Role Description

      The MEL Specialist will design, implement, and manage monitoring, evaluation, and learning systems to track program performance, outcomes, and impact. This role will provide data-driven insights to inform program decisions, improve effectiveness, and ensure accountability.

      Key Responsibilities

      Monitoring and Evaluation

      • Design and implement monitoring frameworks, including indicators, data collection tools, and reporting templates.
      • Develop and manage databases to track program progress and outcomes.
      • Conduct evaluations, including baseline studies, mid-term evaluations, and final evaluations.

      Data Analysis and Reporting

      • Analyze data to identify trends, patterns, and insights.
      • Prepare reports, including progress reports, evaluation reports, and technical reports.
      • Develop data visualizations and dashboards to communicate findings.

      Learning and Capacity Building

      • Facilitate learning events, workshops, and meetings to share findings and insights.
      • Build capacity of staff and partners on MEL concepts, tools, and methodologies.
      • Develop and implement learning plans to improve program effectiveness.

      Qualifications

      Education

      • Master's degree in a relevant field, such as social sciences, evaluation, or statistics.
      • Relevant certifications in MEL, evaluation, or research methods.

      Skills

      • Strong analytical and problem-solving skills.
      • Excellent data analysis, reporting, and communication skills.
      • Experience with MEL frameworks, tools, and methodologies.
      • Ability to work independently and collaboratively.

      Experience

      • Minimum 3-5 years of experience in MEL, evaluation, research, or a related field.
      • Experience in designing and implementing MEL systems.

      • Professional development opportunities.
